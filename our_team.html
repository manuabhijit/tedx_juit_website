Sabal Gupta

I am honored to serve as the lead organizer for TEDxJUIT 2016.
As Curator, my responsibility is to ensure May 22,2016 becomes day of transformation in your life.
TEDx events are independently organized TED-like, multidisciplinary events featuring short talks that seek to highlight the unique passions, ideas, challenges, hopes, and dreams of a specific place or community. So, where better to have a TEDx event, than in a University?
Especially a University that celebrates and supports diverse learners and their needs.

Sabal Gupta

Curator
sabal.gupta1@gmail.com

On Sunday, May 22,
We are pleased to announce that TEDxJUIT 2016's inaugural event will take place at Jaypee University of Information Technology. This half a day event is the culmination of a truly remarkable exercise in collaborative creativity and it is this collaboration that is the hallmark of a TEDx event. My vision, in bringing a TEDx event to JUIT, was to celebrate the ideas worth spreading that come primarily from our student body, but are also reflective of our greater community: parents, alumni members, and educators, who truly try to make a difference in the lives of students.
